一、引言

欢迎使用「心安」应用（以下简称“本应用”）。本应用为个人独立开发、纯本地离线运行的安全守护工具类应用。我们高度重视您的隐私与个人信息安全，特此制定本隐私政策，向您说明本应用如何处理、存储相关信息，以及您享有的权利。

二、信息收集与使用说明

本应用不会向任何服务器、第三方接口、云端数据库上传、收集、共享、出售您的任何个人信息。所有数据仅存储在您的手机本地设备中，应用关闭或卸载后，相关数据将随应用一同清除。

2.1 本地存储信息（仅保存在您的设备）

本应用仅在本地存储以下必要信息：

  • 每日签到时间：用于超时提醒与安全状态判断；
  • 紧急联系人信息（姓名、手机号码）：仅用于一键求助、超时自动求助时唤起系统短信；
  • 情绪记录信息（情绪类型、备注、记录时间）：仅用于您个人查看历史状态。

以上信息仅存在于您的手机本地，不会同步、不会上传、不会被开发者或任何第三方获取。

2.2 权限申请与用途

为实现核心功能，本应用会申请以下系统权限，用途明确且最小必要：

  • 短信权限（SEND_SMS）：用途为一键求助、超时自动求助时唤起系统原生短信界面，帮助您快速发送求助信息；本应用不会在后台静默发送短信，所有短信均需您手动确认发送，应用本身不读取、不上传短信内容。
  • 定位权限：用途为一键求助时获取当前位置经纬度与地图链接，填入求助短信；位置信息仅在本地拼接为短信内容，不会上传、不会保存、不会共享给任何第三方。
  • 通知权限：用途为超时未签到时发送本地提醒通知；通知仅在本地展示，不涉及任何数据上传。

2.3 本应用绝对不会收集的信息

本应用不会收集以下任何信息：

  • 身份证、通讯录、通话记录、短信记录、相册、麦克风、设备唯一标识；
  • 网络行为、浏览记录、位置历史轨迹；
  • 账号、密码、支付信息、社交关系等敏感数据。

三、信息共享与披露

本应用无任何第三方SDK、无广告、无统计、无分析、无后端接口，因此：

  • 不会向任何第三方共享、转让、披露您的任何信息；
  • 不会接入广告、统计、推送、社交分享等第三方服务。

四、数据安全与删除

  • 数据安全：所有信息仅存储在您的设备本地，由手机系统安全机制保护，开发者无法访问；
  • 数据删除：您可在应用内手动删除情绪记录、紧急联系人；卸载应用时，所有本地数据将被彻底删除，不可恢复。

五、未成年人保护

本应用不面向未满14周岁未成年人设计。如您是未成年人，请在监护人同意、陪同下使用本应用。

本隐私政策可能因应用功能调整、平台规则变化不定期更新，更新后的政策将在应用内公示。重大变更将通过应用内提示告知用户。

English Version

1. Introduction

Welcome to the "Xin An" app (the "App"). This App is an independently developed, offline-only safety and well-being tool created by an individual developer. We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information security. This Privacy Policy explains how the App processes and stores information, as well as the rights you have as a user.

2. Information Collection and Use

This App DOES NOT upload, collect, share, or sell any of your personal information to any server, third-party interface, or cloud database. All data is stored locally on your mobile device only and will be deleted when the App is closed or uninstalled.

2.1 Locally Stored Information (Device Only)

The App only stores the following necessary information locally:

  • Daily check-in time: Used for timeout reminders and safety status verification;
  • Emergency contact information (name, phone number): Only used to open the system SMS interface for one-click emergency assistance or automatic timeout assistance;
  • Mood record information (emotion type, notes, recording time): Only for your personal review of historical status.

The above information exists only on your mobile device, and will not be synced, uploaded, or accessed by the developer or any third party.

2.2 Permission Requests and Purposes

To implement core functions, the App will request the following system permissions with clear and minimal purposes:

  • SMS Permission (SEND_SMS): Used to open the native system SMS interface for one-click emergency assistance or automatic timeout assistance to help you send emergency messages quickly; the App will not send SMS in the background without your consent, all SMS require manual confirmation, and the App does not read or upload SMS content.
  • Location Permission: Used to obtain current location coordinates and map links for inclusion in emergency SMS; location information is only used locally to compose SMS content and will not be uploaded, saved, or shared with any third party.
  • Notification Permission: Used to send local reminder notifications when check-in is overdue; notifications are only displayed locally and do not involve any data upload.

2.3 Information We Never Collect

The App will never collect the following information:

  • ID card, address book, call logs, SMS records, photos, microphone, device unique identifiers;
  • Network behavior, browsing history, location tracking;
  • Sensitive data such as accounts, passwords, payment information, social relationships.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

The App contains no third-party SDKs, ads, analytics, or backend APIs. Therefore:

  • We will not share, transfer, or disclose any of your information to any third party;
  • We will not integrate third-party services such as ads, analytics, push notifications, or social sharing.

4. Data Security and Deletion

  • Data Security: All information is stored locally on your device, protected by the mobile system's security mechanisms, and inaccessible to the developer;
  • Data Deletion: You can manually delete mood records and emergency contacts within the App; all local data will be permanently deleted and unrecoverable when the App is uninstalled.

5. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for use by children under the age of 14. If you are a minor, please use the App only with the consent and accompaniment of a guardian.

6. Privacy Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time due to App function adjustments or platform rule changes. Updated policies will be posted within the App. Major changes will be notified to users via in-app prompts.
